Preserving India's Oral Tribal Languages through Speech AI Ongoing
Research proposal submitted to Sarvam AI · Ongoing dataset preprocessing
A proposal for speech-first AI for tribal languages of the Deccan that live only in spoken form and have no script of their own. Rather than forcing such a language into text it never had, the design sends speech through Telugu as a pivot, so reasoning can happen in a language models already handle well, and then carries the answer back to the speaker in their own tongue. The first phase looks at Banjara and Chenchu, one widely spoken and the other close to disappearing.

Ongoing dataset collection
